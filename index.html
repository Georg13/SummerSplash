Summer Splash Festival

Summer Splash Festival is one of the most long-lasting Croatian festivals, ideal for all of those who are lead with goodwill and a desire for a great and relaxing summer vacation right by the sea. The festival offers four days of music on several stages, as well as live music on the beach, free camping, workshops, shops and more. Summer Splash is dancing to early morning hours with the best performers from the region and famous guest names from all over the world. Presenting all of the bass music and promoting sound system culture, Summer Splash has been more than a decade a real treat for all reggae, dub, drum’n’bass, dancehall, ska, and other related electronic music lovers

Be the part of Summer Splash magic – come and see why everybody that comes to this Festival, always returns!
